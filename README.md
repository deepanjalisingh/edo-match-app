# edo-match-app

An hourly tuition booking app for students and teachers

<h3>📚 Hourly Tuition App-</h3>

A modern digital marketplace connecting students with qualified teachers for flexible, hourly-based tuition sessions.

<hr>🚀 Project Overview:</hr>

The Hourly Tuition Platform is designed to simplify the way students find academic support and how educators monetize their expertise.
The platform enables seamless discovery, booking, scheduling, and management of one-on-one tuition sessions.
Our mission is to make quality education accessible, flexible, and transparent.
